Innovationlab at SWM is a dynamic and collaborative space designed to foster creativity, innovation, and knowledge exchange among employees. It comprises two key areas: the Creativity Lab and the Technology Lab.

The InnovationLab is for all SWM employees who are passionate about innovation and creativity, regardless of their role or department. It is a space where individuals can collaborate, explore new ideas, and experiment with emerging technologies. Whether you are a marketer, developer, designer, or project manager, the InnovationLab welcomes you to unleash your creativity and contribute to the culture of innovation at SWM.

Booking the various areas and rooms in the InnovationLab is completely free of charge for SWM employees.
